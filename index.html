Drums are a very essential member of the percussion group of musical instruments. They are normally played by beating with the hand, or with one or two sticks with or without padding.
Depending on the class of drum and the type of sound expected, various sticks can be used. They could be wooden sticks,
brushes or even sticks with a soft beater of felt on the end. On the surface, they appear like instruments that are solely used for music and eventually,
entertainment and for evoking happiness.
